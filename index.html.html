Our Story

Built for students
in Guwahati.

Career Pathways was founded with a simple belief: that every student in Guwahati deserves access to world-class creative education — without having to leave Assam.

We specialise in Adobe Creative Suite training, combining hands-on technical skill-building with dedicated career consultancy. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to sharpen existing skills, our programmes are designed around your goals.

Our approach is straightforward: small batch sizes, certified instructors, real-world projects, and honest guidance on career paths — from freelancing to joining studios, agencies, and media companies.

Certified Instructors Adobe-certified professionals with active industry experience in design and media.
Small Batch Sizes Maximum 10 students per batch so every learner gets personal attention.
Career Consultancy Included Every course includes one-on-one career sessions, resume review, and portfolio guidance.
Local Hiring Network Partnerships with design studios and media companies across Northeast India.
